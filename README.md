# Toby's Games

A collection of fun online games that you can play with your friends!

## Games

### 🏓 Ping Pong
Classic paddle game - play against the computer or challenge a friend!
- 1 Player vs CPU
- 2 Player mode
- First to 11 wins

### ⛳ Mini Golf
Navigate 9 challenging holes with obstacles and hazards
- Click to aim, hold to power up
- Beat your best score
- Sand traps and walls to avoid

## How to Play

Visit [tobyalexanderwoods.github.io/toby-games](https://tobyalexanderwoods.github.io/toby-games) and pick a game!

## Tips

- **Ping Pong**: Use W/S or arrow keys for Player 1, I/K for Player 2
- **Mini Golf**: Click to aim, hold SPACE to build power, release to swing

Have fun! 🎮
